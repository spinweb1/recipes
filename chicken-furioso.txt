1.  Cut up chicken breast to small pieces
2.  Chop up veggies: cabbage, zuchini, etc.
3.  Make brown rice.
4.  Add ginger and soy sauce to taste.
5.  Saute veggies first, then toss in chicken.
6.  Drink wine.
7.  Add rice and saute all together for 7 minutes, stirring frequently.

Voila!  Yummy home made chicken dish.
